Tahini-Lemon Salad Dressing for 100

Makes: 100 servings

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:
  • ½ cup tahini
  • 2 lemons juice of
  • ½ cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil (optional)
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • ½ cups water
 Other Options:
  • apple juice or cider
Equipment:
  • blender
Again this is the same process used when making dressing or 100 salads. Place half of all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. Add more water or lemon or apple juice as necessary, to make a thick, creamy dressing. Repeat.
