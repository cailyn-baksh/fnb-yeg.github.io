    A group of people committed to providing sustainable crisis shelters to the people in need of them in Edmonton and surrounding areas
    Our Initiative
    A team of like minded individuals who go out on our city streets and isolated camps to deliver food, clothing, blankets and survival items to those less fortunate and living rough and advocate for those without a voice.
    YardShare YEG
    A mutual aid organization in Edmonton focused on urban agriculture and facilitating yard sharing.
    Bear Clan Patrol
    Famous for serving the unhoused community with food, laughter and de-escalation tactics, BCP is a Canada-wide Indigenous-led movement.

    YEG Community Response to Covid-19 helps direct people to government resources to help survive the pandemic, and feeds low-income populations in a mutual aid style network.
