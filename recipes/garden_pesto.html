Garden Clean Up Pesto

[view pdf]
Step 1: Gather Garden Greens

  • 3 cups of mixed greens
Today I had to tidy up my container garden and I was able to make pesto with all the greens I pulled out. I had to thin my carrots, pull out some arugula that was starting to bolt, pinch back basil, harvest a couple of radishes and pull out a nasturtium that had gotten too big.
Step 2: Prepare Ingrediants

Cut and chop your garden greens, and gather:

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup of hemp hearts or sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice (juice of 1 lemon)
  • salt to taste
Step 3: Mix and Eat

Mix all together in a food processor until fairly smooth. Add salt to taste.

Serve over pasta or on your next sandwich.

Step 4: Freeze

Tip: Freeze any extra pesto sauce flat so that you can break off chunks.
