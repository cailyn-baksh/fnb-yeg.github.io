SOY Meal
September 18, 2021

Street Outreach YEG program served 50 people at Boyle Street Community Services and George Spady Society tonight! We had a whopping 10 volunteers from Food Not Bombs YEG and Workers' Liberation Coalition. SOY team served pasta, muffins, cake, soup, water, and more. We also had donations of clothing to keep people warm. I am so proud of this initiative and how it's grown in such a short time!

FNB Meal at Boyle Street

Nurses' Info Picket
August 11, 2021

Today's meal: Spinach and cashew-ricotta cannelloni glazed with homemade marinara with a drizzle of vegan mushroom Alfredo sauce, with a side of fresh-picked roasted zucchini parmesan served with garlic bread and solidarity. Nurses, who have been risking their lives to save lives, should be eating better than Jason fucking Kenney. Today, they will be. The zucchinis were grown in our local collective garden, just blocks away from the picket.
