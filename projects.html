Food Not Bombs YEG Helping the Humans Update

  1. FNB YEG is running a program to provide free vegan homemade meals within 4 days of intake.
  2. You will be supplied with an intake form requesting name, phone number, address, number in household, and dietary restrictions.
  3. We will serve you up to 4 times
  4. If you need permanent supports we can help with connection to resources in the city
  5. Let us know if you need food of want to help out!

Food Not Bombs YEG

Community Fridge Launch!

Sept. 23, 2021

FNB YEG is launching our community fridge program! We have acquired a fridge that will be filled with donations that people can take if they need, or contribute to.

The fridge is at Earth's General Store on 82 Ae. If you need more info or want to volunteer to help with the fridge, please message us at Food Not Bombs Edmonton on Facebook or Instagram or at foodnotbombsedmonton@gmail.com

Community Meals

We serve monthly free vegan meals at Beaver Hills House Park. The meals are open to anyone who wants to eat.

Please contact us if you want to help sponsor a meal, donate, or get involved!
